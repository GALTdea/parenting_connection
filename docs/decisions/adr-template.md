# ADR Template

Use lightweight architecture decision records only for decisions that should survive chat history.

Do not create ADRs for small copy changes, helper refactors, minor layout updates, or ordinary implementation details.

## Title

Short decision name.

## Status

Proposed, accepted, superseded, or rejected.

## Date

YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What problem or choice required a durable decision?

## Decision

What did we choose?

## Consequences

What becomes easier, harder, safer, or riskier because of this decision?

## Alternatives Considered

Briefly list serious alternatives and why they were not chosen.

## Follow-Ups

Any work, documentation, or verification needed later.
